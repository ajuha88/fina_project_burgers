The Best Neighborhood in New York - Based on Burgers

Neighborhoods based on quality/quantity of burger joints

Using the foursquare API, we pulled several data points from
burger restaurants in New York to determine the best neighborhood
for a burger enthusiast to live in. The data is plotted on a map of
New York, highlighting the different neighborhoods we looked at.
The map allows you to highlight the neighborhoods based on different
factors, including the average rating and the number of burger joints.
Additionally, you can look at grouped markers for venues specifically
labeled as "burger joints", or for all venues. Clicking on the neighborhoods
also shows the individual statistics for that neighborhood.
